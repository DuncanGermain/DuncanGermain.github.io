Art Design Apparel Writing Video Code Résumé
FRANKENTWIX January 2015 (15 hours)

Ankle-height railings, artificial stepping stones, and low curb-height wooden blocks are all common pieces of equipment in parkour gyms around the world. In this project, I combined all three into a single, versatile, portable training tool. Excellent for balance, precision, strides, broad jumps, and all kinds of physical conditioning.
